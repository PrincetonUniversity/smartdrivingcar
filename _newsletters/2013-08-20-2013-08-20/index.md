          and [Alfred Ely Beach](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alfred_Beach)
          150years ago.  Much

          more interesting than [Musk's Publicity Stunt](http://www.thedailyshow.com/watch/wed-august-14-2013/track-to-the-future).  Also, back in about 1975
          Larry Edwards walked into my office and proclaimed..
          "Alain!!.. I have this great idea of evacuated tube
          transport.  In fact we could build an underground version from
          NYC to SF with a parabolic profile so that we can benefit from
          gravity in both the initial acceleration and the final
          deceleration.  It would be extremely high speed, low energy
          and environmentally attractive.
          To build the tunnel we could detonate low grade nuclear
          weapons to create the tunnel along the way, basically using
          the experience gained in underground nuclear testing."  I just
          shook my head in amazement.  Alain
