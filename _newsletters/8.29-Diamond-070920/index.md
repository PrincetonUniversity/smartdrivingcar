                                                          causing all
                                                          sorts of
                                                          conflict and
                                                          crashes, not
                                                          to mention
                                                          death..."  [Read more](https://www.vice.com/en_us/article/g5pvgm/we-dont-need-cops-to-enforce-traffic-laws)
