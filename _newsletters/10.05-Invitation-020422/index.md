                                                          Princeton SDC
                                                          Summits were
                                                          initiated in
                                                          2017 to
                                                          provide a
                                                          venue for the
                                                          open
                                                          discussion of
                                                          how
                                                          technology, in

                                                          can be shaped

                                                          enjoy one or

                                                          quality
                                                          mobility
                                                          options.

                                                          more
                                                          importantly,
                                                          it became
                                                          obvious that
                                                          substantial
                                                          improvement in
quality-of-life and equitable mobility can readily be made available to

                                                          under-served.

                                                          cannot drive
                                                          themselves,
                                                          cannot afford
                                                          the transport
                                                          alternatives
